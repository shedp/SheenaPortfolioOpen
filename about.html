About

I am an aspiring UX designer and software developer but am open to other opportunities in tech. I have a background in digital marketing with 3 years of agency experience, managing paid search account and contributed to successful product launches on Amazon for challenger brands.

My journey into tech began with a 13-week bootcamp where I gained proficiency in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python, React, Node.js and Express, Flask, Django, Docker, databases (SQL and non-SQL). I worked collaboratively in an agile way, using tools like Trello, Figma and Github.

Most recently, I served as a Collaboration and Productivity IT Specialist for a prominent corporation in the construction. In this role, I oversaw the development and integration of collaboration tools in the enterprise including the M356 estate, end-user documentation and guides for new apps. I was finally able to experience IT in a real-life enterprise environment, engaging with stakeholders across a variety of business units, and working in a SCRUM team.
